Sarveen Cup season 3 registration is now open

Apr 16, 2020

After one year of inactivity, Serveen Games has officially announced season 3 of Sarveen Cup Splendor competitions.

The competition committee has decided to run the competition online due to Corona virus outbreak. The prize pool will be approximately 1000000 IR Rials. Competition registration has already begun and you can sign up by sending a message to the competition administrator.

Sarveen Cup Season 2 is over

Feb 23, 2019

Sarveen cup Splendor competition season 2 is over and Abolfazl Bahari has been named as the new Sarveen cup champion. Earlier today in a breath taking match, Abolfazl Bahari managed to hit the match point in only 7 minutes to take the first place with Golnaz Nikmehr and Ali Zarghami standing in second and third places.

Sarveen Games likes to congratulate Abolfazl Bahari for his glorious victory.

Sarveen cup season 2 has reached its final stage

Feb 18, 2019

With the last match of the group stage being over, we reached the final match of Sarveen cup season 2.

Ali Zarghami managed to come on top of group C to join Golnaz Nikmehr, Hossein Khayami and Abolfazl Bahari in the final.
