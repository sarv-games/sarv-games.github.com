Saeed Mohammadi Ara is the Champion of season 4

Apr 26, 2021

It is finally over. Tonight the final match of Sarveen cup season four was played between the four finalists and Saeed Mohammadi Ara ended Abolfazl Bahari's reign.

This match was the closest final in the history of Sarveen cup where all competitors went toe to toe until the last turn. Ali Zarghami and Abolfazl Bahari showed promising but their performance was not good enough. Saeed Mohammadi Ara who reached the final through playoffs scored 15 before anyone else and named himself the new champion.

Sarveen Games would like to congratulate Saeed Mohammadi Ara for his glorious victory

Sarveen Cup season 3 is over

July 26, 2020

Sarveen Cup season 3 is over and Abolfazl Bahari has managed to retain the trophy.

Earlier today The final match has been held between Abolfazl Bahari, Hossein Ghasemi, Naser Azarbakhs and Ali Zarghami. Abolfazl Bahari managed to score 5 points early in the game which gave him a great head start. Ali Zarghami was having an unbelievable comeback after his terrible opening but Abolfazl was able to keep his lead unitl the end and finish the game in 24 moves.

Sarveen Games would like to congratulate Abolfazl Bahari for his glorious victory.
